Location / How to get to us

By Car

Head to the Visitor Car Park (just off of Stocker Road, Exeter, EX4 4QD). Parking is £10 / day, payable via smartphone app.
Once parked, walk down the road to the Business School

Accessible parking is available directly in front of the Business School

By Train

Stretham Campus is just a short walk up the hill from Exeter St Davids (EXD) train station. Walk along Prince of Wales Road towards the Business School.

On Foot

Follow signs for the Business School, and enter at the XFI centre entrance where our reception desk is. (follow the TechExeter signs and flags!)

By Co Bike

Hop on a Co Bike!
Delegates attending Tech Exeter can travel sustainably to the University of Exeter campus using Co Bikes, the city’s e-bike network. The nearest Co Bikes station to the conference venue is opposite the Into building - minutes away!

TechExeter and Co Bikes have teamed up to offer delegates who are new to Co Bikes 100 free minutes cycling.
To claim your 100 free mins:

  • Call Co Bikes customer service on 01392 961 011
  • Co Bikes will issue you with a voucher code (in exchange for your Eventbrite Ticket Order Number)
  • Download the nextbike app and create an account
  • Redeem voucher code (main menu / add voucher)
  • Enjoy your 100 mins free cycling!

Terms & Conditions

  • Unique voucher code valid for a single redemption
  • New customers still required to pay £10 credit deposit
  • £10 credit deposit pays for first trips once 100 free mins is used
  • Visit www.co-bikes.co.uk for further information
